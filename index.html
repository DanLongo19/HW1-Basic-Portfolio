About Me

My name is Dan Longo and I grew up in Madison, WI. I've lived in Chicago for almost 8 years, and really enjoy the big city. My favorite things to do are go for long runs, try new restaurants and meet up with friends.

I got married last November, and live with my wife and brother in law (he and I were friends before I ever met her, so it's not as weird as it sounds) near the North/Clybourn red line stop. We are looking forward to the summer and when we can walk comfortably around the neighborhood and hang out by the pool.
